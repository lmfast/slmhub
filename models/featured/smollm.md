# SmolLM (featured)

## When SmolLM tends to be a good fit

- Edge/CPU-constrained environments.
- Fast experimentation where you need very low resource usage.

## Practical advice

- Use RAG for “knowledge”.
- Keep prompts short and specify output formats.
