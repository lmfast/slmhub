# Phi (featured)

## When Phi tends to be a good fit

- General assistant-style tasks where you want good quality at lower cost.
- Fast local iteration (especially when paired with quantization).

## First thing to do

Pick a specific checkpoint and validate on your acceptance prompts, then pin the revision.
