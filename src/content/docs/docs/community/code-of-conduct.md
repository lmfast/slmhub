# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Be respectful

- Assume good intent.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Be considerate of differing backgrounds and experience.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private report to the maintainers (recommended) or create an issue with minimal details.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation as needed to keep the community safe and productive.
