# About SLM Hub

## Our Mission

**To democratize AI by making Small Language Models (SLMs) accessible, understandable, and deployable for every developer.**

We believe that the future of AI is not just in massive, centralized models, but in efficient, private, and specialized models running on the edgeâ€”on your laptop, your phone, and your own servers.

## Why We Exist

The AI landscape is dominated by discussions about trillion-parameter models. While impressive, they are often:
- **Expensive** to run.
- **Slow** to interact with.
- **Privacy-invasive** (sending data to the cloud).

SLM Hub exists to show the other side: **Small is Beautiful.**

## The Team

SLM Hub is an open-source initiative maintained by the [LMFast](https://github.com/lmfast) community.
